== Description ==

If you're looking to boost your affiliate marketing efforts on your WordPress site, look no further. Affiliate Reviews allows you to easily create engaging and effective affiliate content using custom blocks that are tailored specifically to your needs. Whether you want to showcase products, casinos, games, forex, etc our plugin makes it easy to generate professional-looking affiliate content that resonates with your audience. And with our visual settings section, you can customize the look and feel of your blocks to match your site's theme and branding, ensuring a cohesive and polished look across your entire site.

== Installation ==

From zip file
1. Upload zip folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

From Plugins search screen
1. Go to Plugins -> Add New
2. Search for "Affiliate Reviews"
3. Click "Install" and "Activate"
